  Key pattern: the split task problem
The most consistent finding is not which tasks are low-value — it's that the same clusters split dramatically by role experience. CourseLeaf: high value (substantive review) vs. lowest value (data entry). Level Up: ranked 2nd by one staff member, 4th of 5 by another. Cross-listing: process ownership (valued) vs. reactive fielding (universally disliked). Before eliminating any task, determine whether the problem is the task itself or how it's distributed.
